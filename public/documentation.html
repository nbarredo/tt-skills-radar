    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions and answers about using the Skills Radar system.

    General Questions

    Q: How do I add a new team member?

    A: Go to the Members page and click "Add Member". Fill in the required information including email, name, hire date, category, location, and availability status.

    Q: How do I import data from Excel?

    A: Use either the Smart File Import (AI-powered) or Excel Import options on the Data Import page. The Smart Import provides better duplicate detection and field mapping.

    Q: How does the AI Assistant work?

    A: The AI Assistant has access to your team data and can answer questions about skills, availability, assignments, and provide business insights. It maintains context across conversations.

    Q: What's the difference between Knowledge Areas and Skill Categories?

    A: Knowledge Areas are high-level groupings (e.g., "Technologies", "Cloud"), while Skill Categories are more specific classifications within those areas (e.g., "Programming Languages", "Frameworks").

    Q: How do I update skill proficiency levels?

    A: Go to a member's profile, click "Edit Profile", navigate to the Skills tab, and update the proficiency levels (1-5 scale) for their skills.

    Q: Can I export data from the system?

    A: Yes, you can export data through the dashboard's "Refresh Data" feature or use the data management tools on the Import page.

    Need additional help? Contact your system administrator or use the AI Assistant for specific questions about your team data.
